How We Worked Together

We collaborated well throughout the project. In our first meeting, we established a GitHub repository for sharing files, allowing updates without manual file transfers. We also used shared documents in Teams so that work could continue even if someone missed a meeting.

We created an in-depth plan with internal milestones, ensuring progress and support for each member's tasks.

What Did We Do to Ensure Team Effectiveness

We held regular meetings to discuss progress and update completed milestones. By keeping similar topics assigned to one person, we minimized research overlap.

We ensured everyone's opinions were considered, especially when deciding on visual content. For roles, we assigned topics based on interest and balanced workloads.
