Data Warehousing- Conor Data Warehousing is an extremely useful modern data management system. It creates an effective way to organize and analyze data helping businesses store large amounts of data. The lack of motivation to clear old data- Conor Clearing old data is expensive and takes time as going through the data and clearing the useless stuff isn't easy creating an apparent lack of motivation to get rid of this existing data. It would cost more time and money to remove the data than just to keep it so most companies would rather keep large databases running to upkeep this data than getting rid of it all together.
